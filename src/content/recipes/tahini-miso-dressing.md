---
title: Tahini miso dressing
pubDate: 2019-04-16
categories: [dressing]
tags: [tahini]
cookingTime: 10
ingredients:
  - 100ml tahini
  - 100ml olive oil
  - 70ml lemon juice
  - 30ml water
  - 2tsp maple syrup
  - 2tbsp white miso
  - 2tsp onion powder
  - 1tsp garlic powder
credit: https://www.bonappetit.com/recipe/tahini-ranch-dressing
---

Mix all ingredients with a stand mixer and season with salt and pepper.
